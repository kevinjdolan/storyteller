## Objective

Make the in-app reading experience usable for longer stories, especially when chapters are present.

## Build

- Allow the user to return easily to planning or audio settings from the reader if they want to make further changes.

## Deliverables

- Comfortable reading layout
- Convenient back-links to earlier stages

- The finalize stage does not trap the user.
- The reader layout feels intentionally designed for reading rather than raw text dump.
